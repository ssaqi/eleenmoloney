INTRODUCTION BIOGRAPHY

James Michael Moody is a lifelong Edgar Rice Burroughs fan and collector. He has contributed over two hundred articles to ERB fan-based publications such as Tarzine, Edgar Rice Burroughs News Dateline, ERBania, The Fantastic Worlds Of ERB and ERB-Amateur Press Association for over forty-five years. In the classic ERB tradition of John Carter of Mars and Carson On Venus Moody presents the first of a three-book Sword & Planet series, Pioneers On Unium, Exiled On Unium and Swordsman On Unium If you are interested in keeping up with the latest Unium series information visit his blog and sign up to receive emails. You can also follow Moody, and the Unium series, on his social media Author Page on Facebook.

BOOK DESCRIPTION

Pioneers On Unium

Book One Of The Unium Series

Pioneers On Unium begins with Howard (Howie) Moody looking for a quiet place to write and get away from the hustle and bustle of New York. He escapes to a cabin in the mountains of North Carolina only to find himself the victim of an alien abduction. With little preamble, Howie finds himself transported aboard an alien craft, part of a group of humans being abducted by a dying race of aliens, the Unium. Having found that the Earth will soon be uninhabitable, the Unium have returned to their home world in search of refuge. Once there, Howie is introduced to a planet full of danger. Unium is a dying planet constantly threatened by asteroid impacts and ferocious creatures that are alien to anything Howie has ever seen. Howie’s situation grows even worse when he learns that the Unium are not friendly and are using the abducted humans for their own purposes. As the survivors struggle to build a new life on Unium, they must fight for survival against both the hostile environment and the Unium’s own internal conflicts. Howie, along with the other abductees, must learn to adapt and find a way to live in this strange new world or die trying. Pioneers On Unium is a gripping tale of adventure and survival that will keep you on the edge of your seat. With nail-biting action, unforgettable characters, and a richly detailed alien world, this book is a must-read for any science fiction fan. Follow Howie’s journey as he navigates this treacherous new land, builds alliances, and fights to protect the people he cares about. With themes of resilience and the power of human spirit, Pioneers On Unium is not just a science fiction novel, but a story about the strength of the human heart and the will to survive. Join Howie and the other pioneers as they forge a new future on a planet full of wonder and danger. Pioneers On Unium will leave you breathless and eager for the next installment in the Unium series.
